My Professional Journey (2024 - 2034)

Over the past decade, I have had the privilege of growing as a professional in the game development industry and beyond. My journey includes significant milestones, from founding my game development studio to launching groundbreaking gamification systems for businesses.

Career Timeline

2024 - Founding My Game Development Studio

In 2024, I established my first game development studio focused on creating cinematic experiences similar to Detroit: Become Human. Our goal was to push the boundaries of storytelling in games.

2026 - First Historical Game: Napoleonic Wars

Our first historical game was based on the Napoleonic Wars, blending interactive storytelling with historically accurate gameplay. The game was praised for its attention to detail and narrative depth.

2028 - Expanding to Gamification Systems

In 2028, I founded my second company, focusing on gamification systems for businesses. We created unique platforms that increased employee engagement and productivity through game-based mechanics.

2030 - Global Recognition for Our Historical Games

Our studio's focus on historical games led to global recognition. By 2030, we had developed games on the Franco-Prussian War and World War I, establishing ourselves as leaders in historically immersive game experiences.

2032 - Revolutionizing Business with Gamification

Our gamification systems evolved, helping major corporations across the world enhance their operations. We introduced AI-powered solutions to tailor gamification to individual employee needs.
